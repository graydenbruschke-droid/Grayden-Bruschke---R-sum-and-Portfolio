Ski Touring Routine

  1. Plan route and destination
  2. Start checking weather forecasts and avalanche reports 2 weeks out
  3. Check safety equipment and gear
    • Beacon
    • Shovel
    • Probe
    • First Aid Kit
    • Helmet
    • Climbing Skins
    • Ski Crampons
    • Bindings
    • Ski Boots
    • Etc
  4. Pack food and water
  5. Inform others where you are going
  6. Have fun!

*Never ski alone and always take precaution when going into the backcountry.
